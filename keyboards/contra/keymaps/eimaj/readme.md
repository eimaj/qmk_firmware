```
,-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------.
 | Esc  |   Q  |   W  |   E  |   R  |   T  |   Y  |   U  |   I  |   O  |   P  | Bksp |
 |------+------+------+------+------+-------------+------+------+------+------+------|
 |Hyp(Tb)|   A  |   S  |   D  |   F  |   G  |   H  |   J  |   K  |   L  |   ;  |  '   |
 |------+------+------+------+------+------|------+------+------+------+------+------|
 | Shift|   Z  |   X  |   C  |   V  |   B  |   N  |   M  |   ,  |   .  |   /  |Sft(Ent)|

 | FUNC | Tab  | Alt  | GUI  |Lower | Sft(Space)  |Raise | Left | Down |  Up  |Right |
```
